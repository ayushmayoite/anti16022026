# Product Overview

## Project Purpose
A sophisticated, classy furniture company website showcasing office furniture products, projects, and services. The website serves as the digital storefront for AFC (Antigravity Furniture Company), presenting an elegant, professional online presence inspired by high-end design aesthetics.

## Value Proposition
- Premium office furniture showcase with elegant, sophisticated design
- Comprehensive product catalog featuring workstations, seating, meeting tables, and soft seating
- Project portfolio demonstrating real-world installations and client work
- Professional brand presence targeting corporate and enterprise clients
- Responsive, accessible, and performance-optimized user experience

## Key Features

### Product Showcase
- Detailed product catalog with multiple categories (workstations, seating, meeting tables, pods)
- High-quality product imagery with gallery views
- Product filtering and categorization
- Individual product detail pages with specifications

### Project Portfolio
- Gallery of completed projects and installations
- Client showcase including major corporate clients (TCS, EY, PepsiCo, Honda, BHEL, Tata Hitachi)
- Project-specific imagery demonstrating furniture in real environments

### Design System
- Comprehensive design token system for consistency
- Sophisticated typography using Playfair Display and Inter fonts
- Elegant color palette with neutral, primary, and accent shades
- Responsive breakpoints (mobile: 768px, tablet: 1024px, desktop: 1280px)
- Fluid typography scaling across devices
- Animation system with hover effects and scroll-triggered animations

### User Experience
- Elegant hero sections with compelling calls-to-action
- Client testimonials and trust indicators
- Partner/client marquee showcasing brand associations
- WhatsApp CTA for direct customer engagement
- Mobile-responsive navigation with mega menu
- Smooth page transitions and micro-interactions

### Technical Capabilities
- Static site generation for optimal performance
- SEO optimization with JSON-LD structured data
- Image optimization with multiple quality levels
- Accessibility compliance (WCAG standards)
- Property-based testing for design consistency
- Comprehensive unit and integration test coverage

## Target Users

### Primary Audience
- Corporate procurement teams seeking office furniture solutions
- Interior designers and architects specifying furniture for projects
- Facility managers planning office renovations or expansions
- Business owners outfitting new office spaces

### Secondary Audience
- Individual professionals seeking premium home office furniture
- Real estate developers furnishing commercial properties
- Government agencies requiring office furniture procurement

## Use Cases

### Product Discovery
Users can browse the complete product catalog, filter by category, view detailed product information, and access high-quality imagery to evaluate furniture options.

### Project Inspiration
Potential clients can explore completed projects to visualize how AFC furniture looks in real office environments and gain confidence in the company's capabilities.

### Contact and Inquiry
Direct engagement through WhatsApp CTA, contact forms, and clear calls-to-action throughout the site enable easy communication with the sales team.

### Brand Evaluation
Corporate clients can assess AFC's credibility through client logos, testimonials, project portfolio, and professional design presentation.

## Business Goals
- Generate qualified leads for office furniture projects
- Establish premium brand positioning in the market
- Showcase product range and quality to corporate decision-makers
- Build trust through social proof (client logos, testimonials, projects)
- Provide seamless user experience across all devices
- Optimize for search engines to increase organic discovery
